Actitud o manera de pensar de quien sostiene que el hombre es por naturaleza superior a la mujer. Se puede encontrar donde sea que ay latinos pero más prominentemente en Latino America forzado por una cultura que siempre a mantenido que la mujer es menor que el hombre

Attitude or way of thinking of those who maintain that man is by nature superior to woman. It is found wherever latinos are but most prominently in Latin America, enforced by a culture that has always maintained that a women is lesser to a man.

MISOGINIA

la misoginia es el rechazo, odio o prejuicio hacia las mujeres producto de diferentes causas como la creencia de que la mujer es el sexo débil e inferior con respecto al sexo masculino

misogyny is the rejection, hatred or prejudice towards women. It is the product of different causes as the belief that women are the weaker and inferior sex with respect to the male sex.

MATA

La muerte violenta de las mujeres por razones de género, tipificada en nuestro sistema penal como feminicidio, es la forma más extrema de violencia contra la mujer y una de las manifestaciones más graves de la discriminación hacia ellas.

The violent murder of women for reasons of gender, classified in our penal system as femicide, is the most extreme form of violence against women and one of the most serious manifestations of discrimination against them.

MUJERES

Mujeres son victimas de Machismo y Misoginia en Latino America, por generaciones han sido maltratadas, abusadas, violadas e incluso asesinadas porque no se valora la vida de una mujer lo mismo como un hombre. Ay mucho trabajo que hacer para asegurarnos que este manera de pensar para de ser normalizado. Necesitamos responsabilizar a los hombres por su continua negligencia de lo peor que trae el machismo. Este proyecto es para poner atencion a la pelea de resitencia de mujeres en Latino America.

Women are victims of machismo and misogyny in Latin America, for generations they've been mistreated, abused, violated and even killed due to a woman's life not being valued the same as a man's life. There's a lot of work to do to ensure this way of thinking stops being normalized. We need to keep men accountable for their continued negligence towards the worst that machismo brings. This project was made to bring attention to the fight of resistence of women in Latin America.

- Can Machismo Be Eradicated? By Jeanette Hernandez

"Femicide is often preceded by other unreported and unpunished crimes such as physical or psychological violence and sexual abuse. In Latin America, women are most likely to be killed by their intimate partners or relatives. Even on official record, partner or ex-partner is often identified as the most common perpetrator, as in Argentina and Peru. Certain social circumstances may also substantially increase case occurrence. In the context of the COVID-19 pandemic, for example, domestic violence and sexual abuse reports surged since the lockdown imposed in many countries across the region, particularly in Colombia and Chile."


- Femicide in Latin America – Statistics & Facts Published by Teresa Romero, Aug 4, 2021

"On February 9, 2020—only days before Valentine’s Day—twenty-five-year-old Ingrid Escamilla was murdered by her partner in Mexico. Photos of her body were splattered on newspapers across the country. Days later, seven-year-old Fatima Aldriguett Antón was tortured and killed, further sparking outrage. Thousands of protestors took to the streets in response to these killings—outside Mexico’s presidential palace"... "Calls for action are growing louder; the protests are calling attention to the Mexican government’s failure to release any plan to prevent femicides, as well as to the media’s coverage of the issue, which they say gives too much space to the abuser’s testimony and not enough respect to the victim’s life and body."
